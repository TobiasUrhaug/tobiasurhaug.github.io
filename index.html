About

O.M.Theorem is a music project rooted in the intersection of analogue texture and electronic structure. Running an independent label, the project spans records, live performances at festivals and clubs, and a continuing series of mixes.

The work moves between the dancefloor and the listening room — patient, considered, and built to last.
